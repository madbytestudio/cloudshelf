Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 27, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information necessary to provide the CloudShelf service:

  • Account Information: When you register, we collect your email address and a secure password hash. This is handled by Supabase Auth.
  • Device Fingerprint: A hardware-bound SHA-256 hash (derived from machine UID and hostname) is stored locally on your device. This is never transmitted to our servers.
  • Encrypted Notes Data: All notes are encrypted with AES-256-GCM before storage. Encrypted files may be synced via Supabase Storage, but we cannot read their contents.
  • Purchase Data: To process book purchases via ToyyibPay, we store a record of your transaction IDs, bill codes, and owned book references.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to:

  • Authenticate you across devices
  • Verify ownership of purchased books
  • Provide app update notifications
  • Bind encryption keys to your device

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage & Encryption

All sensitive data uses encryption at rest:

  • Book PDFs downloaded for offline use are encrypted with a device-bound key
  • User notes are encrypted with AES-256-GCM using a separate key derivation salt
  • Encryption keys are derived via PBKDF2 with 100,000 iterations and never leave your device

4. Third-Party Services

CloudShelf uses the following third-party services:

  • Supabase — Authentication, database, file storage, and app update hosting. Supabase Privacy Policy
  • ToyyibPay — Payment processing for book purchases. We do not store your payment card details; all payment data is handled by ToyyibPay. ToyyibPay Privacy Policy
  • Tauri — Desktop application framework. No data is sent to Tauri.

5. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. CloudShelf provides a built-in account deletion feature accessible from the Profile page in the Reader app. When you delete your account, the following data is permanently wiped from our servers:

  • Your profile and login credentials
  • All notes, bookmarks, and reading progress
  • Device bindings and access permissions to books
  • Any books you have uploaded or created
  • Purchase pins associated with your account

To maintain financial and transaction records, sales data is retained even after account deletion. This includes transaction IDs, bill codes, and purchase history required for auditing and reconciliation purposes. All other personal data is completely removed. Local encrypted data on your device will remain until you manually remove it.

6. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encryption, secure authentication, and device binding. Note that no method of electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or port your data. To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below.
